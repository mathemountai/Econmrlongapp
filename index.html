How to Use & Navigate

üí° Quick Tip: Lost in a module? Simply click the 'E' icon in the top corner of any screen to instantly return to the home dashboard.

The app is built on a Progressive Learning System:

  1. Unlock Stages: There are 4 stages of learning. You unlock the next stage by actively using the study tools and interactive links in your current stage.
  2. Gain Gold Coins: As you study and click links, you accumulate Gold Coins.
  3. Hall of Fame: Once you have enough gold, click the Hall of Fame tokens to enter the Gold Reserves. Here, you can collect legendary economists!
